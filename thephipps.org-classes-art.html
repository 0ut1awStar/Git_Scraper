Creative Together: Holiday Ornament Making Drop-In

Saturday, December 13, 2025
drop in any time btwn noon and 4:00 p.m.
Art Studio One

Children must be accompanied by an adult.
Fee: $8 per ornament

Join us for a festive drop-in event to design and create holiday clay ornaments — for decorating your tree or giving as gifts! Pottery studio instructors will guide you through making hand-made ornaments with stamps, texture tools, and cookie cutters, then painting them with colorful underglazes. We’ll glaze and fire your ornaments after the event, and they'll be ready for pick up before Christmas. Our ornament making drop-in is a great way to get into the holiday spirit!
